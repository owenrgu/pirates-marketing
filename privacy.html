UpBeat Privacy Notice

UpBeat is an early-stage project which is being developed as part of a third year module at Robert Gordon University. This website is only used to understand interest in the product and to contact people who want updates.

What we collect

When you submit the enquiry form we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your organisation/venue name
  • The plan you are interested in

Why we collect it

We collect this information strictly for research and development purposes. We will only use your email to:

  • Reply to your enquiry
  • Ask questions about your needs
  • Contact you when we make progress or launch the product

What we will NOT do

  • We will not sell your data
  • We will not share your data with advertisers
  • We will not send spam or unrelated marketing

Where the data is stored

Form submissions are stored securely using Google Forms and Google Sheets.

Your control

You can request removal of your details at any time by emailing us and we will delete your information.
